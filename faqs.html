How do I set up my new Watchnetic smartwatch?

How do I clean and maintain my digital watch?
To clean your digital watch, use a soft, lint-free cloth slightly dampened with water. Avoid harsh chemicals or submerging the watch unless it's labeled as waterproof. For more tips, read our blog post on How to Care for Your Digital Watch.
How do I track my fitness goals with a Watchnetic smartwatch?
Our fitness smartwatches come equipped with features like heart rate monitoring, step counting, and calorie tracking. Simply sync your watch with the companion app to monitor your progress and achieve your fitness goals.
